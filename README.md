# PokemonRPG

### Movement
- Use **Arrow Keys** to move your character.
- Press **Space** to interact with NPCs and objects.
- Press **Enter** to open the Monster Index.

### Interaction
- Stand near an NPC or interactive object.
- Press **Space** to talk or trigger events.
- Battles start when interacting with trainers or encountering wild monsters in grass.

### Navigation
- Use **Arrow Keys** to move between menu options.
- Press **Space** to confirm a selection.

### Actions Available
- Select a **Monster** from your roster.
- Choose an **Attack** type.
- Choose a **Move** type.
- Use **Defense** actions.
- Attempt to **Catch** a monster when allowed.

### Target Selection
- Use **Arrow Keys** to choose the target monster.
- Press **Space** to execute the selected action.

### Monster Index
- Press **Enter** anytime outside battle to view your collected monsters and their details.

### Healing
- Visit the **Hospital NPC**.
- Interact using **Space** to fully heal your monsters.

### Objectives
- Explore wild grass areas to encounter monsters.
- Catch new monsters and add them to your roster.
- Gain experience and level them up.
- Evolve monsters at higher levels.
- Defeat trainers and gym leaders to progress.
